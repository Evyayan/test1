Our Uncompromising Standards

Founded in 1952, Snac Berry began as a small family operation serving maharajas and nobility. Today, we maintain those same exacting standards for a global clientele.

Each ingredient is sourced from our network of specialty growers, many of whom have supplied us for generations. Our master blenders train for seven years before touching production.

The Snac Berry difference lies in our obsessive attention to detail - from the 72-hour slow roasting process to the hand-applied spice blends that make each batch unique.
